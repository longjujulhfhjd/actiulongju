标题1

The best preparation for tomorrow is doing your best today.对明天做好的准备就是今天做到最好！You cannot improve your past, but you can improve your future. Once time is wasted, life is wasted.你不能改变你的过去，但你可以让你的未来变得更美好。一旦时间浪费了，生命就浪费了
